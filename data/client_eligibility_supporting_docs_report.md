## Consolidated eligibility criteria

The extracted records show that a visitor visa applicant is expected to meet the following criteria:

1. Hold a valid passport or travel document
2. Be in good health
3. Have no criminal or immigration-related convictions
4. Show proof of ties to home country
5. Show intent to leave Canada at the end of the visit
6. Show sufficient financial means for the stay
7. Complete a medical exam if required
8. Provide an invitation letter when applicable

## Consolidated supporting documents

The extracted records indicate the following key supporting documents are commonly required (depending on case type):

1. Passport / travel document
2. Bank statements or proof of funds
3. Invitation letter
4. Employment proof / work evidence
5. Proof of relationship
6. Police certificates
7. Medical documents / medical exam records
8. Proof of residence outside Canada (case-dependent)
